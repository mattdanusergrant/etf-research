Robot Funds

A simple guide to 6 robot & AI investments

First, what is a "fund"?

A fund (also called an ETF) is like a basket of many companies.

Instead of buying just one company, you buy a small slice of many at once. If one company does badly, the others can balance it out. This spreads your risk.

Below are 6 baskets. They all invest in robots and the computer "brains" that run them. Tap a colour below to jump to that group.

🦿 Robot-body funds

These buy companies that build human-shaped robots — the actual robots that walk and use their hands. The most focused, and the riskiest.
